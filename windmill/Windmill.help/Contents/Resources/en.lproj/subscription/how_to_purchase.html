How to purchase

You need to use Windmill on the iPhone to buy a subscription.

A Distribution Subscription enables Windmill on the Mac to distribute your app every time you make a code change. Whether it is the end of a sprint, demo day or keeping your team in the loop, the latest build is available to install on your registered devices using Windmill on the iPhone.

Registered Devices

A device must be registered in your Apple Developer Account before it can install your application.
