Upload an app to iTunes Connect

  1. Make sure your project’s Archive bundle has been created.
  2. Below the Archive bundle, select the "Save" then "Keep in Organiser".
  3. In Xcode, open the Archives organizer. Choose Window > Organizer and click Archives.
  4. In the Archives organizer, select the archive, then click Distribute App.
  5. In the sheet that appears, select “iOS App Store.” to distribute through the App Store as the distribution method, then click Next.
  6. In the next sheet, select Upload, then click Next
