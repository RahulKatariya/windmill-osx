Install the app on a device

  1. Make sure your project’s IPA has been created.
  2. In Xcode, open the Devices and Simulators window. Choose Window > Devices and Simulators, then click Devices.
  3. In the left column, select the device.
  4. Drag the IPA under the Installed Apps in the "No apps installed" section to install the app.
Alternatively.
  1. Make sure your project’s IPA has been created.
  2. Under the IPA, Select "Save..." to store the iOS App file.
  3. In Xcode, open the Devices and Simulators window. Choose Window > Devices and Simulators, then click Devices.
  4. In the left column, select the device.
  5. Under Installed Apps, click the Add button (+).
  6. In the sheet that appears, select the iOS App (IPA) file and click Open.
