Console

While Windmill is running, the console provides you with a live output of what it is currently doing.

In the toolbar, use to show or hide the debug area.

The debug area shows the console with two types of outputs.

  • The raw output, showing every single statement and related information.
  • A distilled output, focusing on what is relevant.
Should the distilled output not provide enough information, make sure to check the raw one. If you believe the distilled output is missing relevant information, please do get in touch.

Tip: You can toggle the debug area by pressing ⇧⌘Y.
