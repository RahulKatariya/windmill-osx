Distribute a build

Before Windmill distributes a build of your application, you need to be logged in with your Apple ID.

  1. Go to Apple menu > System Preferences
  2. Select iCloud
  3. You may need to authenticate. Enter your Apple ID and password. Click Sign In.
  4. Select iCloud Drive
  5. Select "Options..."
  6. Under "Documents", make sure the check box for "Windmill" is on
