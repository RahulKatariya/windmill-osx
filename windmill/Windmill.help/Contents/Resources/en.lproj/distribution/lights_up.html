Lights up

Make sure you are signed in with your Apple ID on your Mac.

As soon as you have an active subscription, Windmill on the Mac will start to distribute your application.

Every time a new commit to your project repository is made, Windmill will distribute a new build for your application. Windmill will only distribute a new build for your application upon a succesful run. This is to ensure that you never end up with a broken version.

Tip: Should the distribution of a build fail (e.g. due to a network error):
  1. Hover over the lights under the distribute stage
  2. Poke the lights once the pointing-hand cursor appears

Windmill will try to distribute the build again.
